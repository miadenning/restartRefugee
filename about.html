About

Refugee Restart provides resources for refugees entering a new area. Links will be updated frequently to provide users easy access to helpful information.

Founded by Ana Carvalho, Jordan Pemberton, and Mia Denning: Refugee Restart establishes a safe space for refugees entering the Atlanta area. We hope to aid individuals and help make the transition into the city smoother.

Feel free to contact us by email: RefugeeRestart@gmail.com/
