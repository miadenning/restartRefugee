"Refugee Restart is a connective platform allowing refugees entering Atlanta a safe space to access all resources and information created by individual organizations."

ABOUT
Welcome to Refugee Restart!! We are an Atlanta-based organization that is working to provide a platform with resources, help, and connections to refugees in the area. Various links will be featured on our website, allowing easy-access to helpful resources. Opportunities and important news will be updated frequently to keep users up-to-date on events.
